# Research Query Playbook

For each output type, run these targeted searches. Replace `[COMPANY]` and `[INDUSTRY]` with the actual values.

---

## Industry Overview

Run all 6 of these:

1. `"[INDUSTRY]" accounts payable invoice processing challenges 2025`
2. `"[INDUSTRY]" finance AP team structure CFO controller`
3. `"[INDUSTRY]" ERP technology stack SAP Oracle 2025`
4. `"[INDUSTRY]" digital transformation finance automation trends 2025 2026`
5. `"[INDUSTRY]" market size revenue growth 2025`
6. `"[INDUSTRY]" procurement supply chain complexity`

**What to extract:**
- Market size and growth rate
- Key sub-segments and product/service categories
- Regulatory bodies and compliance requirements (OSHA, FDA, SEC, etc.)
- End markets served
- What makes AP/invoice processing uniquely complex in this sector
- Typical ERP and AP tools used
- Current digital transformation trends
- Finance org structure norms (centralized vs decentralized AP, shared services)

## Role-Based Outreach

**If industry only (Mode A):** Skip contact name searches. Run searches 5–7 only, replacing [INDUSTRY] for the sector context. Focus on role-specific pain points and pressures in that industry.

**If company provided (Mode B):** Run all 7 searches below.

1. `"[COMPANY]" CFO OR "Chief Financial Officer" finance leadership`
2. `"[COMPANY]" controller OR "Head of Accounting" OR "VP Finance" finance`
3. `"[COMPANY]" "AP Director" OR "AP Manager" OR "Accounts Payable Manager"`
4. `"[COMPANY]" "VP of Finance" OR "Vice President Finance"`
5. `"[COMPANY]" annual revenue employees headquarters industry`
6. `"[COMPANY]" acquisition merger expansion 2024 2025 2026`
7. `"[COMPANY]" news announcement strategy 2025 2026`

**For Mode A — also run:**
- `"[INDUSTRY]" CFO priorities challenges 2025 2026`
- `"[INDUSTRY]" controller "head of accounting" AP pain points`
- `"[INDUSTRY]" "AP director" OR "AP manager" invoice processing challenges`

**What to extract:**
- CFO name and brief background (Mode B only)
- Controller or Head of Accounting name (Mode B only)
- AP Director/Manager name (Mode B only)
- VP of Finance name (Mode B only)
- Company size: revenue, employees, locations (Mode B); typical company profile in sector (Mode A)
- Recent news and strategic signals: acquisitions, expansions, leadership changes, cost initiatives
- Industry/sector and what makes AP complex there
- Known or likely ERP and AP tools

## Full Account Brief

Run all 9 of these:

1. `"[COMPANY]" annual revenue 2024 2025 financials`
2. `"[COMPANY]" CFO controller "VP Finance" "head of accounting" leadership team`
3. `"[COMPANY]" ERP SAP Oracle technology stack`
4. `"[COMPANY]" acquisition 2024 2025 growth strategy`
5. `"[COMPANY]" accounts payable procurement invoice`
6. `"[COMPANY]" news press release 2025 2026`
7. `"[COMPANY]" private equity ownership investors`
8. `"[COMPANY]" locations offices plants employees`
9. `"[COMPANY]" jobs hiring "accounts payable" OR "controller" OR "finance" OR "accounting" site:linkedin.com OR site:indeed.com OR site:glassdoor.com`

**What to extract:**
- Full company snapshot (revenue, employees, HQ, founded, ownership)
- All named executives in finance (CFO, Controller, Head of Accounting, VP Finance, AP Director/Manager)
- Known tech stack (ERP, procurement, AP automation tools)
- AP complexity factors (multi-site, acquisitions, international, product complexity)
- Strategic signals and recent news (last 18 months)
- PE ownership and exit signals if applicable
- Competitive context (what AP tools they might already use)
- **Job postings:** Any open finance/accounting roles — specifically AP Specialist, AP Manager, Controller, Accounting Manager, Finance Analyst. Note role title, seniority level, and any details in the job description about current tools, volume, or pain points. If no relevant open roles found, note that and omit the section from the doc.

## Executive One-Pager

Run all 7 of these:

1. `"[COMPANY]" revenue employees headquarters overview`
2. `"[COMPANY]" CFO "VP Finance" finance leadership`
3. `"[COMPANY]" news acquisition expansion 2025 2026`
4. `"[COMPANY]" ERP technology accounts payable`
5. `"[COMPANY]" growth strategy private equity ownership`
6. `"[COMPANY]" industry sector what they do`
7. `"[INDUSTRY]" finance pressures trends challenges 2025 2026` (use the company's industry)

**What to extract:**
- 3 headline stats (the most impactful: revenue, locations/scale, acquisitions or employees)
- Industry the company operates in
- 3–4 industry-level pressures driving urgency for AP automation (regulatory, margin, labor, M&A, etc.)
- 3–4 company-specific operational facts that create AP complexity
- 3 "why now" signals specific to this company's current situation
- Vic.ai impact statements tied directly to their specific challenges (not generic)

## Tips for Difficult Searches

- If a company is private and financials are hard to find, try: `"[COMPANY]" site:crunchbase.com OR site:owler.com OR site:zoominfo.com`
- For CFO/exec names: try `"[COMPANY]" site:linkedin.com CFO` or `"[COMPANY]" leadership team site:[company].com`
- For recent news: try `"[COMPANY]" site:businesswire.com OR site:prnewswire.com 2025`
- For ERP/tech stack: try `"[COMPANY]" SAP OR Oracle OR "Microsoft Dynamics" OR NetSuite`
- If the company is very small or obscure, use the industry research to fill in context gaps
